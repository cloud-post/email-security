The answer, of course, is choosing the right email sending service. Fortunately, there are plenty of options available, each with its own specialties, strengths and weaknesses. Choosing one based on your needs and business structure should be an informed choice.

Email continues to be one of the most popular forms of communication in business today. In fact, it's estimated that over 89% of companies use email marketing as part of their overall marketing strategy. With so much riding on email marketing, it's important to find an email service provider (ESP) that can guarantee reliable, fast, and secure email delivery. 2. Sendgrid

Sendgrid is one of the oldest Email Service Providers, and that allows them to be of service throughout the entire world. Their deliverability is quite good, which may appeal to businesses worldwide.

3. Sparkpost

Sparkpost have been the closest competition to Sendgrid for a long time, and they had excellent customer service with a trendy price tag as well. Before recently, Sparkpost management increased their pricing by 1.5 to 2 times per month, prompting many online solutions to observe them as either money monger or truly an operator of email delivery.

4. Mailgun

Mailgun enjoys delivering an Email Service for Developers through its efforts.

They have dedicated application programmer interfaces (APIs) for you to integrate the SMTP Server, and they generously provide relevant analytics and detailed reports. The Email Verification utility was a crucial step in Mailgun's campaign to steadily boost email quality. In recent years we see that they have become increasingly concerned with their Bulk Emailing and Burst Sending campaigns because of the impression this leaves on their deliverability.

5. Mandrill App

Another notable Email Sending Service, Mandrill exists for quite a while. But once it was purchased by Mailchimp, it did not take long to see that users were concerned only about their marketing, and not the logistics of delivery.

Their excessive increase in pricing proves that they, too, have greatly changed.
