Get Maximum Benefit From Your Email Marketing Strategy By Utilizing An SMTP Relay Service


An SMTP relay is a service that allows the transmission of email messages from one server to another. You can use an analogy for an SMTP relay service to picture an Olympic relay race: a transactional or promotional marketing email is the baton, and SMTP servers are runners who pass the baton to each other until the last relay line.

If you don't want to use an SMTP relay provider to send emails, you may set up your own email server to ensure that the emails your staff members send a pass through or can be received. In such instances, you're accountable for setting up and maintaining your mail infrastructure, building a good email reputation, and dealing with email bounces and spam complaints.

There are a few different types of SMTP servers, but the most common is the SMTP relay server. SMTP relay servers are used to relay messages from one email server to another. In most cases, SMTP relay servers are used to relay messages from an email client to an email server.
