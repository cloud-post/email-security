Here are 6 top selections for free email providers:

1. HubSpot Email Hosting

HubSpot email hosting permits you to take your promotions to the next level. Email hosting is part of HubSpot's Marketing Hub, and you have access to all of HubSpot's tools to manage your communications. You can create beautiful and stylish emails with a drag-and-drop editor.

HubSpot permits you to send as many as 2,000 pieces of email at no additional charge, with additional deliveries available starting from $45 per month. HubSpot allows you to create campaigns and hone in on particular mailing lists, allowing you to put your CTAs where they will be most effective. You can also examine the effectiveness of your CTAs using A/B testing on your emails.

2. Gmail

Gmail is Google's email service, and it grew into one of the most reliable and versatile email services available over time. After you log into Gmail, you will enjoy 15 GB of free email storage to start with, and it comes with a number of helpful features that make it easier to manage it all.As you type your email and check the correspondence history, you'll be able to determine all communication threads related to a particular message. Gmail additionally features a powerful search capacity that permits you to look for specific messages.

3. Outlook.com

Most people have heard of Microsoft Outlook as the application component of Microsoft Office, but may not realize that it has a complete set of email services as well. Microsoft rebranded Hotmail, an archive email service, into Outlook.com and more broadly updated it, making it one of the most useful email services available.

4. iCloud Mail

iCloud Mail is a simpler system, but can still address the requirements of a company owner looking for an extremely streamlined email service. There are no ads, and additionally, due to the fact that it was created by Apple, it's extremely optimized for use on mobile devices including the ever-loved iPhone. People of iPhone and iPad Mail will be delighted with iCloud Mail's interface.

5. Zoho Mail

Zoho Mail is a targeted service for additional office features, such as those offered by advanced search and a detailed labeling function. A limited Zoho Mail account has 5 GB of storage and can be upgraded to 10 GB for no extra charge. Zoho Mail also has an integrated Zoho Chat and advanced capabilities for Google Docs.

6. Yahoo! Mail

Yahoo!. Mail is one of the oldest, most established free email services around today, and it has some impressive features. Messages in this free email account have unlimited volume and sent files of up to 50 megabytes (or 100 MB total size). You can organize received emails into folders as desired, and Yahoo! Mail even has a built- in search function to search for messages you've sent, received, or sent.
