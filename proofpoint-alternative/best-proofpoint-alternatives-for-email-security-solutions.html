Best Proofpoint Alternatives For Email Security Solutions


Email security is a top concern for businesses of all sizes. With the ever-growing list of data breaches, it's more important than ever to have a robust email security solution in place.

There are a number of email security solutions on the market, and it can be tough to decide which one is right for your business. 

Securing your email starts with choosing the right gateway. As a marketing manager or business owner, you may understand the dangers of utilizing the mail gateway alone. Even though Proofpoint is still a well-established and resilient mail gateway, it's worth considering other options. In this blog post, we'll take a look at some of the best Proofpoint alternatives for email security solutions.

DuoCircle is at the top of DuoCircle's list of email security service providers. DuoCircle's email security services protect against phishing attacks, ransomware, and malware attacks, spam, and spoofing and impersonation detection. As a cloud-native email security gateway, Trustwave is known for its exceptional management and visibility into security resources.
