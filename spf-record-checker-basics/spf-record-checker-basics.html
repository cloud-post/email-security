Protect Your Business: SPF Record Checker Basics


As a business owner, protecting your brand and securing your online presence is paramount. One critical aspect of doing this is setting up and managing an SPF (Sender Policy Framework) record. An SPF record is a DNS record that lists which IP addresses are allowed to send emails on behalf of your domain. It helps to prevent email spoofing, or the sending of unauthorized messages from a domain that appears to be legitimate.

What Is An Spf Record?


Sender Policy Framework record (SPF record) is the keystone of an SPF implementation, in which the SPF policy is defined. An SPF record is published in the DNS (Domain Name Service) and contains a list of authorized email servers (mail servers authorized by the registrar) for your domain.If the email address for the sender of an email is not listed as an alternative record and the sender of the email is utilizing your domain to send email, this email may be discounted within your email address.
